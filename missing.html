Missing People


Madeleine McCann


Name: Madeleine McCann

Born: May 12, 2003, Leicester, United Kingdom

Siblings: Amelie McCann, Sean McCann

Parents: Gerry McCann, Kate McCann

Grandparents: Susan Healy, Brian Healy

Story: Madeleine McCann vanished while on holiday in Portugal with her family in May 2007. Her parents, Kate and Gerry, were at one time considered formal suspects by the Portuguese police. More than a decade on, this cold case continues to attract headlines from the world's media and Netflix.
